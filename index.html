Timothy Odonga

Data Scientist

Biography

Timothy is a data scientist who is currently working with Dalberg Data Insights, a company focusing on building inclusive data ecosystems to address international development challenges.

Outside of work, his research interests broadly encompass machine learning, and computer vision and focus on building trustworthy and reliable systems to assist humans. Some research areas of interest include:

  1. Algorithmic fairness, model explainability, uncertainty quantification, and robustness to dataset shift.
  2. Efficient algorithms for resource constrained settings such as scarcity of well curated data, limited compute resources.
